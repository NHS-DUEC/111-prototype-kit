This is a prototype developed by a team within NHS England's Digital Urgent and Emergency Care directorate who are working to solve the following problems:

  • Reduce the number of avoidable emergency prescription referrals from 111 online.
  • Prevent intentional and unintentional overuse of emergency prescription referrals.
