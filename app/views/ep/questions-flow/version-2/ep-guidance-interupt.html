Help save NHS money

The emergency prescription service is here for you when you need it but you can help save the NHS money if you get your medicine with a standard repeat prescription.

You could set a reminder in your phone or on a calendar to remember to order your repeat prescription.

You can order it using:

  • the NHS app
  • your GP surgery website
  • your pharmacy app

Allow extra time for your prescription to be approved during busy times, like Easter, Christmas and New Year.

Other ways to order your prescription

If you have difficulty contacting your GP surgery, you can order your repeat prescription using:

  • the NHS app
  • your GP surgery website
  • your pharmacy app

Remember to allow extra time for your prescription to be approved during public or bank holidays, like Christmas and New Year.
