When you contact the pharmacy

You need to tell them you were referred by 111.

Tell the pharmacist what medicine you need and when the next dose is due.

The pharmacist will then tell you:

  • if they have it in stock
  • if it is safe to give you the medicine
  • what to do if they cannot give you the medicine

Getting the medicine sent to you

If the pharmacist can supply the medicine they will arrange to deliver it to you.

The medicine should turn up in time for you to take your next dose. If it does not, contact the pharmacy again.

You will not have to pay for the delivery.

If the medicine is out of stock

The pharmacist will contact another service for you. It is the pharmacist's responsibility to help you get the medicine you need.
